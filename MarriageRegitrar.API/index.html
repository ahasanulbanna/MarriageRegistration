About Crypto Credit

Crypto Credit (CCD) is a decentralized cryptocurrency token issued by Crypto Credit Foundation. The founders of Crypto Credit Foundation are vastly experienced in NGO funding since the last 15 years. They have funded more than 200 NGOs all over the world, as a non profitable organization.
The key focusing areas are Health sector, Education sector, IT sector, Technical & Vocational training programs, Micro-credit programs, Ensuring human rights etc.

Our Vision

★ The main vision of Crypto Credit is to create successful small and medium entrepreneurs by providing them loan facilities in low interest rate against easy conditions and involving them in social business.

★ To eliminate unemployment by creating new jobs with micro credit programs using blockchain technology.

★ To work on human resources development.

★ To work on protection of the rights of children & women.

★To Facilitate expansion of medical treatment.

★ To facilitate expansion of social business by operating microcredit program using blockchain technology.

★ To build up CCD coin users community group.
